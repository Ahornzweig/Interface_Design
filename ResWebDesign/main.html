Under this motto we from IVY & OAK partnered with The Pokémon Company to spread our love for sustainability through designes that make you fall in love with and wear our pieces beyond a season, because sustainability essentially means longevity. This Campaign centers around the eternal beauty of people who have embraced themselves. So we decided to take inspration from the embrace Pokémon Gardevoir.
Gardevoir represents elegance and compassion in a way we find inspiring. So we want to show you how we envision this strong Pokémon in the hopes to show you how important it is to embrace yourself.
